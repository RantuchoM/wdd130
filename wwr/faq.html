Is rafting suitable for team-building activities in companies? What are the benefits?

Rafting is an excellent activity for team building in companies. It offers a unique opportunity for teams to work together, communicate effectively, and develop trust and camaraderie. The challenges of navigating the rapids require collaboration and coordination, fostering teamwork and problem-solving skills. Moreover, the breathtaking natural surroundings create a conducive environment for team bonding and personal growth.

What is the minimum age requirement for participants in team-building rafting trips?

We welcome participants of all ages on our team-building rafting trips. While there is no strict age limit, we have found that participants aged 8 and above tend to fully enjoy and engage in the experience. However, we can tailor the trip to accommodate younger children as well, ensuring a safe and enjoyable adventure for everyone.

Is whitewater rafting a safe activity for non-swimmers?

Absolutely! Whitewater rafting is suitable for both swimmers and non-swimmers. Our guides are highly trained and certified in Wilderness First Aid, with extensive experience in rafting. They will provide comprehensive safety instructions before the trip, including what to do in the event of falling out of the raft. Additionally, all participants are provided with coast guard approved life vests or personal flotation devices (PFDs) to ensure their safety throughout the adventure.

What is included in a team-building rafting trip?

Our team-building rafting trips include all necessary camping equipment for a comfortable stay. This includes sleeping bags, foam pads, waterproof duffle bags, small waterproof day bags, tents, cots, eating utensils, and camping chairs. During the day, we provide water and lemonade, and in the morning and evening hours, we offer coffee, tea, and hot chocolate. Participants are welcome to bring their own beverages such as beer, liquor, and soft drinks (within reasonable amounts). We also provide three meals per day, along with snacks to keep everyone energized.

What should participants bring for a team-building rafting trip?

To ensure a comfortable and enjoyable experience, we recommend participants bring the following items:

  • Rain jacket and rain pants (avoid ponchos)
  • Quick-dry shirts and shorts
  • Long-sleeve cotton shirts and pants (helps keep cool on hot days)
  • Light-weight cotton pants, nylon pants, or sarongs
  • Swim trunks (for men) or swimsuits/quick-dry underwear and sport bras (for women)
  • Hat with a large brim for sun protection
  • Sunglasses with straps
  • Socks (for hiking and sun protection)
  • Sandals or water shoes (or hiking shoes if preferred)
  • Comfortable clothing for camp activities
  • Sleepwear
  • Water bottle
  • Small day pack for hikes
  • Moisturizing lotion
  • Headlamp or flashlight
  • Moleskin for blisters
  • Waterproof sunscreen and lip balm

Optional items:

  • Gloves
  • Fleece jacket
  • Towel
  • Bandana
  • Small pillow
  • Hygiene products (toothpaste, toothbrush, tissue, nail clippers, etc.)
  • Extra eyeglasses or contacts
  • Biodegradable soap and shampoo
  • Camera equipment
  • Book, watercolors, journal, and pen

What makes whitewater rafting an exceptional activity for team-building purposes?

Whitewater rafting offers a thrilling and unforgettable experience that goes beyond traditional team-building activities. It allows team members to step out of their comfort zones, face challenges together, and build trust and communication skills in a dynamic and exciting environment. Each day on the river presents a combination of thrilling rapids, calm stretches, scenic hikes, and opportunities for learning about the history and geology of the area. After an adventurous day, participants can relax by the river, engage in conversations, and enjoy delicious meals prepared by our guides. The serene nights under the starry sky add a touch of tranquility, making the experience truly remarkable.

How far in advance should companies book their team-building rafting trip?

To secure specific dates, we recommend booking your team-building rafting trip at least one year in advance. However, if you have some flexibility with your dates, booking six months in advance is usually sufficient to ensure availability and proper arrangements for your team.
